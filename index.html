Desktop Injection Molding for Makers

A compact injection molding concept designed for makers, small shops, and engineers.

A concept under development by engineering students at CMU

The Idea

Traditional injection molding is powerful but expensive and inaccessible. This concept aims to bring that capability to a desktop-sized machine for prototyping and small production.
