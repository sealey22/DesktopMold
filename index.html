The Idea

Traditional injection molding is powerful but expensive and inaccessible. This concept aims to bring that capability to a desktop-sized machine for prototyping and small production.

Compact Footprint

Designed as a desktop-sized concept for workshops, labs, and maker spaces.

Rapid Iteration

Intended to help users move beyond slow print cycles and test molded parts more efficiently.

Accessible Manufacturing

Built around the idea of making small-batch plastic part production more approachable.
